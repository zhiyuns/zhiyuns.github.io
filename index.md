## Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student in [Department of Computing](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/computing/), [Imperial College London](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/) under the supervision of [A/Prof. Wenjia Bai](https://www.doc.ic.ac.uk/~wbai/web/). 
Before joining Imperial, I obtained Master's degree at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) and Bachelor’s degree at [Beihang University](https://www.buaa.edu.cn/).
I worked as a research intern in [Deepwise](https://www.deepwise.com/) from December 2021 to June 2022, and in [Alibaba DAMO Academy](https://damo.alibaba.com/?lang=en) from November 2024 to Octorber 2025. 

My research interests lies in generative models and their applications in medical image analysis, including self-supervised learning, modality synthesis and super-resolution in medical images. I am also working on the development of multi-modal analysis, segmentation, and pretraining algorithms for medical images.
